# Design Context — SciReagent

## Target Audience
Research scientists and laboratory procurement specialists at universities, research institutes, and biotech companies worldwide.

## Use Cases
- Searching for reagents by CAS number, product name, or catalog number
- Browsing products by modification type (8-Oxo, 2-Thio, 6-Thio)
- Comparing specifications (purity, molecular weight, formula, storage conditions)
- Selecting package sizes and adding to cart
- Requesting quotes for bulk orders
- Downloading Datasheets, MSDS, and Certificates of Analysis

## Brand Personality
- Professional & rigorous — reflects the precision of biochemical research
- Trustworthy — credible scientific information, no marketing fluff
- Distinctively modern biomedical — molecular aesthetics, clean data presentation
- Global — English-first, accessible to international researchers

## Design Direction
- **Tone**: Refined scientific minimalism. Clean, data-dense but readable. Let the chemistry speak.
- **Palette**: Deep biomedical greens as primary. Neutral grays for structure. High contrast for data legibility.
- **Typography**: Modern grotesque for UI, monospace for chemical data (CAS numbers, formulas).
- **Differentiation**: Molecular structure patterns as subtle decorative elements. Data-forward product cards that feel like mini datasheets, not e-commerce cards.
